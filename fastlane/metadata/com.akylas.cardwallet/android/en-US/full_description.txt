<p><i>OSS Card Wallet</i> is a free and Open Source app to transform your mobile device into a powerful card scanner and wallet.</p>

<p><b>SCAN</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Scan your IDs, business cards, and loyalty cards with a fast camera scan.</li>
  <li>Enjoy the automatic edge detection feature allowing you to quickly scan many documents.</li>
  <li>Bulk scan to quickly add recto/verso (front/back) sides.</li>
  <li>Import cards from images or even existing PDFs.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>BARCODE</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Enjoy automatic detection of barcodes, QR codes, and more.</li>
  <li>Manually create a card with a specific barcode.</li>
  <li>Add as many barcodes as you want to a single card.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>PASSBOOK</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Get full Passbook support.</li>
  <li>Import directly from .pkpass files.</li>
  <li>View your Passbooks exactly as you would in the official Apple Passbook App.</li>
  <li>Export and share your passbooks as .pkpass or PDF files.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>ENHANCE</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Improve PDF quality by applying enhancing filters for color or black & white cards.</li>
  <li>Edit crops seamlessly.</li>
  <li>Reorder pages and merge cards.</li>
  <li>Rotate pages easily.</li>
  <li>Change brightness and contrast to create beautiful, clear cards.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>ORGANIZE</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Create color-coded folders for quick and easy access.</li>
  <li>Sort cards efficiently.</li>
  <li>Custom name your cards.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>EXPORT</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Create PDFs directly from your mobile device by converting photos, images, and other documents into beautiful, configurable PDFs.</li>
  <li>Export and share your cards as PDF, JPEG, or PNG with friends, family, and colleagues.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>SEARCH</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>OCR your cards using offline, open-source models in many languages (requires a one-time download).</li>
  <li>Generate searchable PDFs by converting images to text using the powerful OCR models from the Tesseract project.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>SECURITY</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Improve the security of your generated PDFs by adding a secure password.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>SYNC</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Synchronize your cards, PDFs, and images with your computers, other devices, or local folder sync.</li>
  <li>Sync seamlessly using WebDAV, Google Drive, or OneDrive.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>PRIVACY</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Use a completely free and Open Source card wallet designed explicitly to protect your privacy.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>COMMUNITY</b></p>
